About Me

I am an ambitious student currently doing my PhD in computer science at Aarhus University. I work in the research group Algorithms, Data Structures and Foundations of Machine Learning with Professor Kasper Green Larsen as my supervisor. The PhD focuses on achieving better theoretical understanding of classic learning algorithms. In my spare time, I like doing competitive programming, honing my skills by solving and implementing a variety of challenging algorithmic problems.
